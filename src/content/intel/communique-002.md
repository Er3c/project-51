### Acquisition Modalities

1.  **Lump-Sum Directives**: A proposed direct financial injection to the Danish Crown, significantly exceeding the territory's GDP.
2.  **Citizen Stipends**: A controversial but highly effective "signing bonus" for every registered resident of Greenland, effectively bypassing political deadlock by appealing directly to the populace.
3.  **Perpetual Lease**: A 99-year sovereign lease model, similar to the Panama Canal Zone or Hong Kong, but with full federal integration rights.
